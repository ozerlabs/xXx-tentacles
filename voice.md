## Mechanics

- ~50/50 lowercase vs sentence-case starts; brands mostly lowercased (blinkit, phonepe, bangalore), acronyms lowercased (otp, sms, ota); selective caps (Apple, INOX)
- Short tweets drop the terminal period; longer reflective ones keep it
- The ".." two-dot beat before a wry tag line; space before "?" sometimes; "???" for incredulity
- Occasional dropped apostrophe ("dont", "doesnt") coexisting with correct ones — leave a few in, don't force them
- Bimodal length: one-word hype ("banger", "HITT") ↔ 40-word single-breath run-on musings ("I sometime wonder…")
- Flat declarative debunks, zero hedging ("This is fake.", "phonepe already tried", "You don't need sms permission to read otp")

## Never

Emojis. Hashtags. Links. Threads. Exclamation marks. Em dashes. "imo"/"I think" hedges on corrections. Dunking on individuals. Engagement bait. Self-promo.
